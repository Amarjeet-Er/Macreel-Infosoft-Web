Day 4 is dedicated to system testing and quality checks, followed by Day 5, where final data verification and access role assignments are completed. On Day 6, we distribute staff credentials and provide onboarding resources. Day 7 includes live staff training sessions for hands-on module usage. By Day 8+, the system is fully functional—parent portals are activated, and our 24/5 support team ensures your ERP runs smoothly from day one.
